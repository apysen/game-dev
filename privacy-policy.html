Privacy Policy

For the mobile game Knight's Gauntlet

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the game Knight's Gauntlet ("the game", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. The game is developed and operated by Apy Sen. By playing the game you agree to this policy.

Knight's Gauntlet has no user accounts and asks for no sign-in. The only information that ever leaves your device is the display name you choose and the score you achieve, which appear on a public leaderboard.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Display name. Before playing, you choose a short name (up to seven characters). This name is shown publicly on the online leaderboard next to your scores. You are not required to use your real name, and we ask that you do not enter your real name or any other information that could identify you.

Game data sent to our leaderboard

  • Scores you achieve in the relevant game modes.
  • Equipped skin (a cosmetic choice shown beside your name).

Your display name, score, and equipped skin are sent to and stored on our leaderboard database so that scores can be ranked and shown to other players. Online leaderboards are limited to certain difficulty and daily-challenge modes; other scores are kept only on your device.

Information stored only on your device

The following is saved locally on your device (using your device's standard app storage) and is not transmitted to us:

  • Your in-game progress, personal high scores, and settings.
  • In-game currency, owned and equipped cosmetic skins, and achievements.
  • Daily-challenge attempts used.

This local data is removed if you delete the game or clear its data.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your email address, phone number, or real name.
  • We do not require or create an account or password.
  • We do not collect your contacts, photos, location, or files.
  • We do not include third-party advertising, and we do not use advertising identifiers or tracking technologies.

2. How We Use Information

We use the display name, score, and equipped skin solely to operate the online leaderboard — ranking scores and displaying them to other players — and to keep the leaderboard free of inappropriate names. We do not use this information for advertising or profiling, and we do not sell it.

3. The Public Leaderboard

The leaderboard is public by design: any player can see the names, scores, and equipped skins of other players. Please keep this in mind when choosing a name. The game includes a filter that blocks inappropriate names and a way to report names that are offensive; reported names may be removed.

4. Service Providers

We use the following third-party service to operate the game:

  • Supabase — provides the hosted database that stores and serves leaderboard entries (display name, score, equipped skin). Their handling of data is governed by their privacy policy at https://supabase.com/privacy.

The game is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain information at the platform level (for example, for app delivery, crash reporting, and basic statistics) in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This is separate from the data the game itself collects.

5. Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with third parties except with the service provider named above for the sole purpose of operating the leaderboard, or where required by law.

6. Data Security

Information sent to the leaderboard is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but because the only data we transmit is a self-chosen name and a score, the privacy impact of this information is low.

7. Data Retention and Your Choices

  • On-device data: delete the game or clear its data to remove everything stored locally.
  • Leaderboard data: to request removal of a leaderboard entry associated with your display name, contact us at the email below and tell us the name and mode. You can also report an inappropriate name from within the game.

8. Children's Privacy

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the display name you enter appears publicly, players should not enter a real name or any personal details. If you believe a child has submitted information that identifies them (for example, a real name used as a leaderboard name) and you would like it removed, contact us at the email below and we will remove it.

9. International Users

The game may be played in, and data may be processed in, countries other than your own. By using the game you consent to the processing of the limited information described above. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access or delete information that relates to you; you can exercise these by contacting us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes may also be noted within the game.
